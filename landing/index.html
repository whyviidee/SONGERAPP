How it works

Three steps.
Your music, owned.

01

Search or paste

Type a song, artist, or album name. Or paste a Spotify, Tidal, SoundCloud, or Bandcamp URL directly.

02

Pick your format

Choose MP3 at 320kbps, 256kbps, or 128kbps. Download a single track or an entire playlist as a ZIP.

03

It's yours forever

Files land in your music folder with proper tags, cover art, and artist/album structure. No internet needed after.

Features

Search anywhere

Spotify or Tidal, your choice. Browse playlists, liked songs, albums, and artists. Switch services anytime.

Real files, real quality

320kbps MP3 with ID3 tags, album art, and proper file naming. A file you own, not a cached stream.

Entire playlists at once

Download full playlists as a ZIP. Duplicate detection and download history built in.

Any URL, any source

Paste a SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Mixcloud, or YouTube link. SONGER extracts the audio directly — no Spotify needed. 1000+ sites supported via yt-dlp.

Mac & Windows

Native app for macOS Apple Silicon, macOS Intel, and Windows. One codebase, works on your machine.

Auto updates

One-click updates. SONGER installs them without leaving the app.

Trending charts

8 genres. What's hot right now, ready to download.

Yours forever

No login wall. No internet after download. Close the app, your music stays.
